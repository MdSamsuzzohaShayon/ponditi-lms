## 3. Design Instructions for Implementation

* **Spacing:** Maintain generous white space (padding of at least 100px between major sections) to prevent the page from feeling cluttered.
* **Shadows:** Use very soft, subtle drop shadows on the course cards and the testimonial cards to create depth without looking dated.
* **Buttons:** Apply a slight border-radius (around 4-6px) to buttons and use a "hover" state that darkens the mint green slightly.
* **Image Styling:** Use high-resolution photography with a consistent bright, warm lighting style. Incorporate the "blob" or "geometric" masks for the profile images.
